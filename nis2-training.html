NIS2 Mandatory Training for Management Bodies

Structured training to ensure your board and senior management meet the cybersecurity knowledge requirements mandated by the NIS2 Directive.

What this service will offer

NIS2 Article 20 requires management bodies of essential and important entities to undergo cybersecurity training. This service will provide tailored, expert-led training programmes designed for executives and board members.

  • Training aligned with NIS2 Article 20 requirements
  • Tailored content for boards, C-suite, and senior management
  • Led by cybersecurity experts with regulatory experience
  • Practical, scenario-based approach — not generic awareness
  • Documentation and certification of completed training
  • Available as on-site, remote, or self-paced formats
