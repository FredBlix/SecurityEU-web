How It Works

Four steps. One email to start.

Step 01

Send

Forward the security questionnaire, contract appendix, or compliance checklist to us via email. Any format.

Step 02

Analyze

Our AI agents parse, classify, and draft responses grounded in your documented controls and governance framework.

Step 03

Review

Every response is reviewed by a qualified cybersecurity professional for accuracy, completeness, and regulatory alignment.

Step 04

Deliver

You receive audit-ready responses within your agreed SLA — with a summary of findings and any recommended follow-ups.

Our Approach

AI built from real expertise, not generic models

Most platforms feed questionnaires into general-purpose AI and hope for the best. We took a fundamentally different approach.

Decades of expertise, systematically codified

Our AI system was built from decades of hands-on experience responding to security requirements for large corporations, critical infrastructure operators, and government agencies. Real-world response patterns, decision frameworks, and regulatory interpretation methods developed over thousands of assessments — codified into specialized AI skills.

Purpose-built through structured knowledge transfer

Our lead expert — a PhD in cybersecurity and former CISO for large-scale organizations and security-critical government agencies — systematically transferred that expertise into purpose-built AI agents through a structured training program. Not fine-tuned on the internet. Trained on how this work is actually done.

AI that works like an experienced professional

The result is an AI system that works the way a senior security professional works — applying proven methodology, understanding regulatory context, and knowing what assessors actually look for. Every response is then reviewed by qualified cybersecurity professionals before delivery.
