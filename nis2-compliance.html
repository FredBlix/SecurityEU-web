NIS2 Information Security Policy Compliance Check

Automated assessment of your information security policies against NIS2 requirements — with actionable recommendations to close gaps.

What this service will offer

NIS2 mandates robust information security policies for essential and important entities. This service will analyse your existing policies, identify compliance gaps, and provide clear recommendations for remediation.

  • Automated policy analysis against NIS2 Article 21 requirements
  • Gap identification with severity and priority ratings
  • Actionable remediation recommendations
  • Mapping to ISO 27001 and other frameworks
  • Compliance readiness report for management and auditors
  • Ongoing monitoring as policies evolve
